    How to play?

    Spider Solitaire is a card game where the goal is to arrange all cards in descending order (from King to Ace) of the same suit and clear them from the tableau

    Rules

    • Card Movement

      Cards can be placed on a card of the next higher rank (e.g., 6 on 7).

      A sequence of cards to be moved must be of the same suit and in descending order (e.g., 9♥, 8♥, 7♥).

    • Empty Columns

      An empty column can accept any card or valid sequence.

    • Revealing Cards

      Moving cards that uncover a face-down card automatically flips it face-up.

    • Stock

      When no moves are left, deal 10 new cards from the stock (one per column). All columns must have at least one card to do this.

    • Completing Sequences

      A full sequence (K, Q, J, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, A of one suit) is automatically removed from the tableau.

    • Difficulty Levels

      Easy: 1 suit (e.g., all spades).

      Medium: 2 suits.

      Hard: 4 suits (standard game).

    Tips

    Plan moves to uncover face-down cards and create empty columns for more flexibility.

    If you make wrong move you can use CTRL + Z keys combination or just press the button "undo" in menu
